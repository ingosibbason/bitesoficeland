Rhubarb and crowberry jam
Urta

Icelandic rhubarb and crowberry jam is a traditional Icelandic delicacy made by cooking rhubarb and crowberries together with sugar and water until the mixture thickens. The resulting jam has a sweet and tart taste and is commonly used as a spread or topping. It is a popular souvenir for tourists visiting Iceland and represents the unique flavors of the region.
