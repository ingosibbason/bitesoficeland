Rhubarb and crowberry jam
Urta

Icelandic rhubarb and crowberry jam is a traditional Icelandic delicacy made by cooking rhubarb and crowberries together with sugar and water until the mixture thickens. The resulting jam has a sweet and tart taste and is commonly used as a spread or topping. It is a popular souvenir for tourists visiting Iceland and represents the unique flavors of the region.

Hangikjöt
Company name

Hangikjöt is a traditional Icelandic dish made from smoked, cured and dried lamb meat, typically obtained from the leg or shoulder of the animal. The meat is smoked over birch wood, giving it a smoky flavor and aroma. It can be stored for several months, and is usually boiled or steamed before serving hot with boiled potatoes, white sauce, and peas. Hangikjöt is an important part of Icelandic culinary heritage and is often served as part of a traditional Icelandic Christmas dinner.
